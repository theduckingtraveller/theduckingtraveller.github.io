Amrita Savadatti

Hey there!

Welcome to my little corner of the internet — a place where I share stories from my travels around the world. By day, I’m an engineer, but my heart has always belonged to exploring new places, meeting people, and collecting moments (and a few misadventures too!). I’ve been dreaming of putting my travel tales into words for ages, and I figured there’s no better time than now to finally do it. I hope these stories make you smile, inspire you to pack your bags, and maybe even help you plan your own adventures a little better.

The world feels smaller than ever, and it’s amazing how a plane ride (or sometimes just a brave leap!) can take you somewhere you never imagined you’d be. I’ve been lucky enough to experience a few of those magical moments, and I’m excited to share them with fellow wanderers like you.

So, grab a cup of coffee (or a glass of wine!) and enjoy reading about my travel escapades. Here’s to new journeys and endless stories! 🌍✈️✨

Thanks for tuning in!
